# Swift Sword — Development Showcase

The visual evolution of Swift Sword, from a one-file Street Fighter clone to a
GPU-rendered samurai fighting game. Every image is a real in-game screenshot.

- **`swiftsword_evolution.png`** — full 14-milestone timeline montage.
- **`swiftsword_before_after.png`** — Day One vs Today side-by-side.
- **`milestones/`** — the individual screenshots, numbered in build order.

## The journey
1. Day One — code-drawn "Street Fighter 2" (procedural stick fighters)
2. Muscular redesign + castle stage + SF2 HUD
3. Bigger fighters + the caped hero APEX
4. Real pixel-art sprite fighters (KAITO & KENJI) in a painted forest
5. Special moves (energy waves, dash & rising slashes, blade-synced hits)
6. Pre-fight presentation: VS splash, kata demo, spoken countdown
7. Mortal-Kombat-style blood + victory kata
8. Weather (leaves / rain / storm + lightning) and slow-motion KO cinematics
9. Renamed to **Swift Sword**
10. Mobile touch controls + installable offline PWA
11. Roster of 4 (KAITO, KENJI, RONIN, ONI), 3 stages, Arcade & Training modes
12. PixiJS WebGL rendering — CRT scanlines + bloom
13. GPU effects layer — embers, energy glows, spark bursts, shockwaves
14. Today — full composite of particles + bloom + CRT over hand-tuned combat
